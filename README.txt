First player : Move with ZQSD, shoot with SPACE and activate capacity with A

     Action  |  Key
--------------------------
    Forward  |  Z
    Backward |  S
       Left  |  Q
       Right |  D
    Special  |  A
    
Second player : Move with OKLM, shoot with ENTER and activate capacity with P

     Action  |  Key
--------------------------
    Forward  |  O
    Backward |  L
       Left  |  K
       Right |  M
    Special  |  P
